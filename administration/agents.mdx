The Agents page shows every agent on your team — who they are, what they do, and how they relate to each other. It's your org chart.

Violet sits at the top as Director of AI, coordinating the entire team. Below her, agents are grouped by department:

- **Sales** — Piper (Business Development), Scout (Competitive Research)

Use the **+** and **−** controls at the bottom-right of the org chart to zoom in and out. The fullscreen button expands the chart for easier navigation on large teams.

## Filtering your team

At the top of the page, filter between:

- **All** — Every agent, including custom ones you've created.
- **Appy.AI** — The pre-built specialist agents that come with your account.
- **Custom** — Agents you've hired yourself for roles specific to your business.

## Adding a new agent

To hire a custom agent, click the **+** button. You'll be prompted to give them a name, role, and behavior instructions. Once created, they appear in your org chart under the department you assign them to.

<Note>
  Want to shape how an existing agent behaves? See [Customizing Your Team](/your-ai-team/customizing-your-team) for instructions, knowledge, and behavior settings.
</Note>
