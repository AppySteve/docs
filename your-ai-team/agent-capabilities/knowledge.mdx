---
title: "Knowledge"
description: "Share your expertise with your agents — documents, guides, and references they draw on naturally."
---

Knowledge is what makes your agents truly yours — the difference between generic answers and responses that reflect your actual perspective, methods, and expertise.

Share documents, frameworks, and references, and your agents absorb them. They surface the right information at the right time without you having to point them to it.

Here's how businesses use knowledge:

- A **consulting firm** uploads service descriptions, process docs, and case study summaries — agents reference the right materials when building client deliverables
- A **coach** shares their proprietary framework and exercise workbook — agents guide clients through the actual methodology, not generic advice
- A **small business** adds their employee handbook and SOPs — agents follow the real procedures when answering questions

Your agents support documents (markdown, text, and PDF files) and images (PNG, JPG, GIF). Smaller, focused files work better than one massive document — if you have a large handbook, consider splitting it by topic.

The easiest way to add knowledge is to tell Appy.AI directly:

- "Here's our brand style guide — add this to my agents' knowledge"
- "My agents should reference this product catalog when answering questions about our lineup"

<Tip>
In most cases, just uploading a file is enough — your agents will reference it when it's relevant, automatically.
</Tip>
