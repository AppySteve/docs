---
title: "Integrations"
description: "Connect your agents to the 900+ tools your business already uses — securely, with scoped permissions."
---

Integrations let your agents work inside your existing stack — reading data, taking actions, and interacting with other platforms on your behalf. Connect once and your agents handle the rest.

Connect with over 900 applications — including HubSpot, Slack, Salesforce, Google Docs, Google Calendar, Gmail, Google Drive, Google Sheets, Jira, GitHub, Linear, Shopify, Stripe, Intercom, and more. For each integration, your agents get access to specific actions: reading emails, creating calendar events, updating records, sending messages.

When a task requires an integration, your agent walks you through connecting your account. Authorize the service once, and your agents can work with it from then on. Disconnect any integration at any time.

Your agents never see or store your passwords. Each operates within defined, scoped permissions — no blanket access.
