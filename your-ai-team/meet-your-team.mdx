Your AI team works the way a great team works: each one has a specialty, they hand work off when it makes sense, and you don't have to manage the details. You talk to Violet — she dispatches the rest.

New to this? See [Working with your team](/your-ai-team/working-with-your-team) for how to brief them, approve plans, and iterate on results.

Twelve specialists, organized the way you'd organize a small business. Each one delivers a real artifact — a doc, a spreadsheet, a forecast, an email — not just advice. Pick the job, brief the team, approve the plan, and let it run.
