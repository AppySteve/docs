# Installation

Install Appy.AI in Slack or use the web platform to start working with your AI team. Setup takes just a few minutes.

## Slack

Slack is the primary way to work with Appy.AI. Once installed, you can message your AI team directly in Slack like any other colleague.

**To get started:**

1. Install the Appy.AI app from the Slack App Directory or through your Appy.AI workspace
2. Authorize the app to access your Slack workspace
3. You're ready to go — Appy.AI appears as a member in your workspace

Once installed, open a direct message with Appy.AI and start delegating. There's no additional configuration required. Just describe what you need, and your AI team gets to work.

<Info>
Appy.AI only accesses conversations where it's directly mentioned or messaged. Your other Slack conversations remain private.
</Info>

## Web platform

The web platform is always available and provides the same conversational interface you get in Slack, plus access to advanced settings.

**Use the web platform to:**

- Message your AI team when you're not in Slack
- Manage your agents and view the full roster
- Configure individual agent settings
- Upload knowledge files to give your team more context
- Set up triggers and automations
- Access organization settings

You don't need the web platform to get started — Slack is enough for day-to-day work. But when you want more control or need to configure advanced features, the web platform is where you'll find it.

To access the web platform, sign in at the Appy.AI website with the same account you used to create your workspace.

<Info>
More messaging platforms are coming soon. Appy.AI will meet your team wherever they already work.
</Info>
