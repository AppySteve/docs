[Appy.ai](http://Appy.ai) operates within Slack and Microsoft Teams, allowing you access to your AI team members right where you work.\
\
When you first sign up, you'll create an [Appy.ai](http://Appy.ai) account using your Microsoft or Slack Log-in. You'll get immediate access to the platform and can start using Appy.AI right away. Yo

## Slack

Appy.AI lives in Slack as an app you install in your workspace — right where your team already operates. Once it's there, you can message it just like any other team member.

To get started, select to sign up via Slack. You'll authorize the app to access your workspace, and that's it, you're ready to go.

From there, open a direct message with Appy.AI and start telling Violet what you need. No commands to memorize, no special syntax. Just describe your goal like you would to a colleague - Violet takes it from there.

<Tip>
  Use the two characters -- or the word 'aside' in a thread with [Appy.ai](http://Appy.ai) and she will ignore your command. Great for use in channels where you might want to discuss with other team members.\
  \
  _aside @steve are you following this conversation with Appy? Redirect her if you would like!_ 
</Tip>

## Microsoft Teams

Appy.AI works directly inside Microsoft Teams as a custom app you install in your workspace. Once it's there, your team can message your AI agents without leaving Teams.

Getting set up requires a Microsoft global administrator to upload the app and grant permissions. The process takes about 10 minutes.

## Inviting Your Human Coworkers

Once [Appy.ai](http://Appy.ai) is installed within Slack or Teams, it is available for anyone in your organization. (Note: Microsoft Teams allows you to restrict access via app permissions).

<Tip>
  You can add [Appy.ai](http://Appy.ai) to a channel for public use, or encourage team members to find her in Applications to begin their own direct message
</Tip>
