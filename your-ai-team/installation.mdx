# Installation

Get Appy.AI running where your team already works.

## Slack

Appy.AI is a Slack app you install in your workspace. The setup is straightforward: install the app, authorize it, and you're ready to go.

Once installed, Appy.AI appears as a member in your workspace. You can message Appy.AI directly like any other team member.

Just open a direct message and start describing what you need. Your AI team gets to work immediately.

## Web platform

The web platform is always available at the Appy.AI website. It gives you the same conversational interface plus access to advanced settings.

Use it to manage agents, configure settings, and upload knowledge files. The web platform is where you'll find deeper controls when you need them.

You don't need it to get started — Slack is enough for day-to-day use. But it's there whenever you want more control over your AI team.

<Info>
More messaging platforms are coming soon. Slack and the web platform are available at launch.
</Info>
