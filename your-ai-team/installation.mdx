# Installation

Get Appy.AI running where your team already works.

## Slack

Appy.AI is a Slack app you install in your workspace. The general flow is simple: install the app, authorize it, and you're ready to go.

Once installed, Appy.AI appears as a member in your workspace. You can message Appy.AI directly like any other team member — no special commands or syntax required.

## Web Platform

The web platform is always available at the Appy.AI website. You'll find the same conversational interface plus access to advanced settings.

Use it to manage agents, configure settings, and upload knowledge files. That said, you don't need it to get started — Slack is enough for day-to-day use.

<Info>More messaging platforms are coming soon. Slack and the web platform are available at launch.</Info>
