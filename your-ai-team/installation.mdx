# Installation

Install Appy.AI in Slack or use the web platform to start working with your AI team. This article covers setup for each supported platform.

## Slack

Connecting Slack brings Appy.AI into the workspace where your team already communicates. Once connected, your AI team members can join channels, respond to messages, and collaborate alongside your human team.

### Connect Slack to Appy.AI

To connect your Slack workspace:

1. Open the Appy.AI web platform and click the organization switcher in the top navigation
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu
3. Navigate to the **Integrations** tab
4. Find the Slack integration card and click **Connect**
5. Follow the prompts to authorize Appy.AI in your Slack workspace

<Info>
You need to be an organization admin to connect or disconnect Slack.
</Info>

### Disconnect Slack

If you need to remove the Slack connection:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** in the organization switcher
2. Find the Slack integration card
3. Click **Disconnect** to remove the workspace connection

Disconnecting Slack will remove Appy.AI's access to your workspace. You can reconnect at any time by following the connection steps above.
