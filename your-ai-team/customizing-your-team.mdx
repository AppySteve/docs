# Customizing Your Team

Your AI team starts ready to work, but it gets better the more it knows about your business. No forms or settings required — just conversation.

## Refining agents through conversation

The easiest way to customize your team is to tell Appy.AI about your business. Share context naturally, and agent behavior adjusts automatically.

**Examples of what you might share:**

- "Our brand voice is casual and friendly — we never sound corporate"
- "We always include a call-to-action at the end of blog posts"
- "Our target audience is small business owners in the health and wellness space"
- "We refer to our customers as 'members,' not 'users'"
- "Our main competitor is Acme Corp — keep an eye on what they're doing"

The more context you provide over time, the better your team performs. You don't need to share everything upfront. Just mention things as they come up, and your agents learn.

This works because Appy.AI remembers what you've shared and applies it across your entire team. Tell it once, and every agent knows.

## Creating new agents

If you need a specialist that doesn't exist yet, just describe the role. Appy.AI creates the agent for you.

**Example:** "I need an agent that handles customer onboarding emails"

That's it. Appy.AI builds the agent based on your description. The new agent inherits your organizational context — it already knows about your brand voice, your audience, and everything else you've shared.

**More examples of agents you might create:**

- "Create an agent that monitors our social media mentions and summarizes sentiment weekly"
- "I need a specialist for writing product descriptions for our e-commerce store"
- "Build an agent that drafts responses to common customer support questions"

No technical skills required. If you can describe the job, you can create the agent.

## Growing your team

Start with the 8 pre-built agents and the basics. As your business evolves, your AI team evolves with it.

**A natural progression:**

1. Start by delegating everyday tasks to the agents you have
2. Share context about your business as you go
3. Create new specialists when you notice gaps
4. Set up autonomous work for recurring tasks

Your AI team scales with your business. No hiring, no training, no onboarding. When you need more capacity or new capabilities, just ask.
