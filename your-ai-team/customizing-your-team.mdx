# Customizing Your Team

Your AI team starts ready to work, but it gets better the more it knows about your business.

## Refining Agents Through Conversation

The easiest way to customize your team is to just tell Appy.AI about your business in Slack. Share what matters, and your agents adjust automatically.

**Here's what you might say:**

- "Our brand voice is casual and friendly — never corporate"
- "We always include a CTA at the end of blog posts"
- "Our target audience is small business owners in health and wellness"
- "When writing outreach emails, always mention our free trial"
- "We use AP style for all written content"

Appy.AI adjusts agent behavior based on what you share. The more context you provide over time, the better your team performs.

No forms or settings — just conversation.

## Creating New Agents

If you need a specialist that doesn't exist yet, describe the role to Appy.AI.

**Examples:**

- "I need an agent that handles customer onboarding emails"
- "Create an agent that monitors our social media mentions and gives me a weekly report"

Appy.AI creates it — no forms, no code.

New agents inherit your organizational context. They already know your business, your brand voice, and everything else you've shared.

If you can describe the job, you can create the agent.

## Growing Your Team

Start with the 8 pre-built agents and the basics. Add specialists as new needs emerge.

Your AI team scales with your business — no hiring, no training.

Power users can fine-tune individual agents through the web platform's agent settings.

For advanced configuration — like uploading knowledge files, setting up triggers, or connecting integrations — visit the agent settings section on the web platform.
