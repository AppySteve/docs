# Customizing Your Team

Your AI team starts ready to work, but it gets better the more it knows about your business.

## Refining Agents Through Conversation

Tell Appy.AI about your business in Slack, and agents adjust their work accordingly. No forms, just conversation.

Here are some examples:

- "Our brand voice is casual and friendly — never corporate"
- "We always include a CTA at the end of blog posts"
- "Our target audience is small business owners in health and wellness"
- "When writing outreach emails, always mention our free trial"
- "We use AP style for all written content"

The more context you share over time, the better your agents perform.

## Creating New Agents

Need a specialist that isn't part of the pre-built team? Just describe the role to Appy.AI, and it creates the agent for you.

Examples include "I need an agent that handles customer onboarding emails" or "Create an agent that monitors our social media mentions and gives me a weekly report." No forms, no code. New agents inherit your business context automatically.

## Growing Your Team

You start with 8 pre-built agents, and you can add specialists as needs emerge. Your team scales with your business — no hiring, no training required.

Power users can fine-tune agent behavior through the web platform's agent settings section.

<Info>For advanced configuration — like uploading knowledge files, setting up triggers, or connecting integrations — visit the agent settings section on the web platform.</Info>
