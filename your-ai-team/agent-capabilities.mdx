Your AI team is more capable than you might expect. Not a chatbot — Appy.AI is a sophisticated platform and network of agents built for how your business actually operates. Agent capabilities are the underlying building blocks each specialist uses to do real work: the files they produce, when they run, how they're shaped, what they know, what they connect to, and the structured data they work against.
