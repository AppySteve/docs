# Working with Your Team

The core Appy.AI experience is simple: tell your AI team what you need the same way you'd message a colleague.

## Delegating Tasks

Message Appy.AI in Slack with what you need, in plain language. No special syntax required.

You don't need to know which agent handles what — Appy.AI routes the work automatically. Here are some examples:

- "Draft a follow-up email for the client meeting yesterday"
- "What are the SEO opportunities for our homepage?"
- "Research what our top 3 competitors are doing on social media"
- "Summarize the team standup recording and pull out action items"
- "Create a header image for our blog post about spring promotions"
- "Write a cold outreach email for the leads list I uploaded"

Results come back in the same conversation.

## How Agents Work Together

Some tasks need multiple specialists. When that happens, they coordinate automatically behind the scenes.

For example, if you ask Appy.AI to "Create a blog post about our new product," the Content Strategist drafts the article, the Senior SEO Strategist optimizes it for search, and the Image Creation Specialist creates a header image. You get the final result without managing any handoffs.

Another example: preparing a client pitch. The Competitive Researcher gathers market intelligence, the Brand Strategist shapes the messaging, and the Technical Writer formats the final deck. You just ask for the pitch — the team handles the rest.

## Autonomous Work

Agents can run on schedules or in the background without you asking. This is perfect for recurring work that doesn't need your input every time.

Examples include weekly competitive research digests, daily content summaries, and automated meeting summaries. Set it up once through triggers in agent settings on the web platform, and results arrive automatically in Slack.

## Checking In on Results

Results arrive in Slack, and you can ask follow-ups in the same thread. Iterate naturally with messages like "Make it more concise," "Add a section about pricing," or "Use a more casual tone."

The web platform shows agent activity and history if you want a broader view of what's been done.
