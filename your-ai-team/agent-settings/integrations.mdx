Integrations connect your agents to the tools your business already uses.

## How Integrations Work

Integrations let agents read from and write to external services. Once connected, agents can pull data, trigger actions, and stay in sync with your existing tools.

Instead of manually copying information between systems, your agents handle it automatically. They can check your CRM, update your project management tool, or pull data from your accounting software — all without you lifting a finger.

## Connecting an Integration

Configure integrations through agent settings on the web platform. Select the integration you want to add, authorize access to your account, and your agents can start using it immediately.

The authorization process is secure and follows standard OAuth protocols. You control exactly what data and actions each integration can access.

## What Agents Can Do With Integrations

Once connected, your agents can do powerful things:

**Access data from connected tools** — Pull customer information from your CRM, retrieve project status from your PM tool, or check inventory levels from your e-commerce platform.

**Take actions in external services** — Create tasks, send emails, update records, or trigger workflows in your connected tools.

**Keep information in sync across platforms** — When something changes in one system, your agents can update related information in other tools automatically.
