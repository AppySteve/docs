# Configuration

Configuration covers workspace-level settings that affect how your entire Appy.AI environment operates.

## What Configuration Covers

Configuration includes settings that apply across your whole workspace, not just individual agents. These affect how your AI team operates as a whole.

Think of configuration as the foundation that all your agents build on. Changes here ripple through your entire setup.

## Workspace-Level vs. Agent-Level

Agent settings apply to one agent. Configuration applies to everything.

Think of it as the difference between adjusting one team member's role vs. changing a company-wide policy. Agent settings are personal. Configuration is universal.

## Accessing Configuration

Configuration is available through the web platform under workspace settings. Look for workspace or configuration options in the main navigation.

From there, you can review and adjust settings that affect your entire Appy.AI environment.

<Info>
Configuration settings affect all agents in your workspace. Changes here apply broadly, so review carefully before making updates.
</Info>
