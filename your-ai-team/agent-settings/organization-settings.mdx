# Organization Settings

Organization settings cover account-level controls — team members, billing, and organizational preferences.

## What Organization Settings Cover

Organization settings handle the administrative side of your Appy.AI account. This is where you manage who has access, handle billing, and set organizational preferences.

These settings sit above workspace and agent settings. They affect your entire account and everyone in your organization.

## Key Areas

Here are the main areas covered by organization settings:

**Team members** — Invite and manage people in your organization. Control who has access to your Appy.AI account and what they can do.

**Billing** — Manage your subscription and payment methods. View invoices, update payment information, and adjust your plan.

**Organizational preferences** — Account-wide settings that affect your entire organization. These apply to all workspaces and agents under your account.

## Accessing Organization Settings

Organization settings are available through the web platform. Look for organization or account settings in the navigation.

You'll typically find these options in a dropdown menu or settings panel at the account level.

<Info>
Organization settings are typically managed by account administrators. If you don't see certain options, check with your admin.
</Info>
