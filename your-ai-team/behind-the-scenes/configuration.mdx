Configuration settings control how your entire Appy.AI workspace operates. These are workspace-level settings that apply across all your agents.

## What configuration covers

Configuration includes settings that apply across your whole workspace, not to individual agents.

This is where you control how your AI team operates as a whole.

Think of it as the difference between adjusting one team member's role versus changing a company-wide policy.

## Workspace-level vs agent-level

**Agent settings** affect one specific agent. You might change one agent's name or model.

**Configuration** affects everything. Changes here apply to all agents and how your workspace functions.

When you adjust configuration settings, you're making decisions that impact your entire AI team.

## Accessing configuration

You access configuration through the Appy.AI web platform.

Look for workspace settings or configuration in your navigation.

This is typically where you'll find system-wide preferences and operational controls.

<Info>Configuration settings affect all agents. Changes apply broadly, so review carefully.</Info>
