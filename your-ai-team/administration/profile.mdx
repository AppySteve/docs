title: "Profile"
---

Your profile and account settings live in a modal accessible from your avatar at the bottom-left of the navigation. There are three tabs: **Profile**, **Organization**, and **Members**.

## Profile

The Profile tab is where you manage your personal account information.

**What you can update:**

- **Profile photo** — Click the photo to upload a new image.
- **First and last name** — Edit directly in the fields and save.
- **Email addresses** — Your primary email is shown with a `Primary` badge. Add additional addresses using the input field and **Add** button.
- **Password** — Click **Set password** to create or update your login password.
- **Connected accounts** — Link third-party sign-in providers (e.g., Google) for one-click login. If none are connected, this section reads "No connected accounts."

## Organization

The Organization tab shows your organization's name and public URL slug. These fields are read-only — contact [support@appy.ai](mailto:support@appy.ai) if you need to change your organization name.

If you need to leave the organization entirely, the **Leave Organization** button is available at the bottom of this tab.

## Members

The Members tab shows everyone who has access to your Appy.AI organization. From here you can:

- See all current members and their roles
- Invite new team members by email
- Remove access for members who have left

<Note>
  Only organization admins can invite or remove members. If you need to change your role, contact your org admin or reach out to [support@appy.ai](mailto:support@appy.ai).
</Note>
