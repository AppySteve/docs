The Billing page is where you manage your Appy.AI subscription and payment method.

## How billing works

Appy.AI starts free — your first 20 tasks are on us.\
\
After that, it's **\$199/month** for unlimited access on the **AI Workforce** plan. \
Cancel anytime, no commitment.

## Adding a payment method

A credit card is required to access Appy.ai. If you did not add a credit card during account creation, or need to change your card information.

1. Go to **Billing** in the left navigation.
2. Enter your card details, or pay with Apple Pay.
3. Save your payment information to activate your subscription.

You won't be charged until you choose to upgrade.

**Accepted payment methods:**

- Credit or debit card — Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Apple Pay

## Managing your subscription

Once your payment method is on file, the Billing page allows you to upgrade, manage your subscription, and cancel.

## Canceling your subscription

Click **Manage Subscription** and follow the cancellation steps. Your access continues through the end of the current billing period. There are no cancellation fees.
