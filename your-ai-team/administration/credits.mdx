Shared workspace credits give your team the flexibility to pay for exactly as much AI work as you need — scale up when demand increases, scale back when it eases. No per-seat charges, no fixed overhead for seats that aren't delivering value.

If you're hitting the ceiling of your free trial, this page explains how credits work and how to choose the right plan.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are credits?">
    Credits represent the real cost of AI work. Think of it this way: work you'd pay a contractor $1,000 to do might cost $100 in credits. Credits aren't arbitrary units — they reflect actual model costs, which Appy subsidizes so your team gets more output per dollar than you'd get going direct.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many credits does a task use?">
    It depends on what the task involves:

    - **Quick tasks** (summaries, lookups, short drafts, Slack responses): ~100–500 credits
    - **Research reports, multi-step workflows, longer deliverables**: ~1,000–3,000 credits

    Credit usage broken down by task is available at [builder.appy.ai/billing](https://builder.appy.ai/billing).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are credits shared across my team?">
    Yes — credits belong to the workspace. All team members draw from the same pool. Per-member and per-department limits are on the roadmap for a future update.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do credits roll over?">
    Monthly plan credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. Reward credits — earned through referrals and other programs — never expire and are applied before plan credits each month.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when credits run out?">
    Any in-progress task completes. Once the balance hits zero, your workspace is notified and new tasks are paused until you upgrade to the next plan tier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add credits mid-cycle without changing plans?">
    Not directly. To get more credits before your next renewal, upgrade to the next plan tier — the prorated difference will be reflected in billing. Downgrading takes effect at the start of the next cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I monitor usage?">
    Credit usage — broken down by team member and task — is available at [builder.appy.ai/billing](https://builder.appy.ai/billing).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
