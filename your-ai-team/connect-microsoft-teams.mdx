Connect Appy.AI to Microsoft Teams to start working with your agents directly in your Teams workspace.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- Microsoft Teams admin access to upload custom apps
- Global admin permissions to grant app consent
- An active Appy.AI organization

## Step 1: Enable custom app uploads

In the Teams admin center, enable custom app uploads for your organization:

1. Go to **Teams admin center** > **Teams apps** > **Setup policies**
2. Select your policy (or create a new one)
3. Turn **Upload custom apps** to **On**
4. Save your changes

## Step 2: Start connection flow in Appy.AI

From your Appy.AI web platform:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Integrations**
2. Find the Microsoft Teams card
3. Click **Connect Teams**
4. Click **Download App** to download the app zip file

Keep this wizard open — you'll need it in the next steps.

## Step 3: Upload app in Teams admin center

Upload the Appy.AI app to your Teams workspace:

1. Go to **Teams admin center** > **Teams apps** > **Manage apps**
2. Click **Actions** > **Upload new app**
3. Select the zip file you downloaded
4. Wait for the upload to complete

## Step 4: Grant admin permissions

Grant the necessary permissions for Appy.AI to work in your workspace:

1. In the Appy.AI connection wizard, click the consent screen link
2. Review the requested permissions
3. Click **Accept** to grant consent

You must be a global admin to complete this step.

## Step 5: Open app in Teams

Open the Appy.AI app in your Teams workspace:

1. In Microsoft Teams, go to **Apps**
2. Select **Built for your org**
3. Find and click **Appy.AI**
4. Click **Open** to launch the app

## Step 6: Connect your organization

Complete the connection by linking your Appy.AI organization:

1. Copy the full `/connect` command from the connection wizard
2. Open a direct message with the Appy.AI bot in Teams
3. Paste the command and send it
4. Verify the connection is successful

You're now ready to start working with your AI team in Microsoft Teams.

## Tips

- **Use direct messages**: DM the Appy.AI bot for the best experience. Direct messages support file uploads, while channels may have limitations.
- **One-time setup**: Only one person needs to run the `/connect` command to link your organization.
- **File delivery**: Files from Appy.AI are delivered as download links in Teams.

## Troubleshooting

**App not showing up after upload**

Sign out of Teams and sign back in. The app should appear in your **Built for your org** section.

**Connection command not working**

Make sure you copied the full `/connect` command from the wizard, including the workspace token. The command should look like `/connect <your-token>`.

**Permission consent failing**

The admin granting consent must have global admin permissions in your Microsoft 365 organization. If you don't have these permissions, contact your IT administrator.
