## Meet Violet — your Director of Appy.ai

Every team needs a leader. Violet is Appy's. She's the Director who coordinates your entire AI team — receiving your goals, building a plan, and delegating work to the right specialists automatically.

Working with Violet:

1. **Learn your business** — Violet will understand business goals to align with you on what you need.
2. **Approve the plan** — Review Violet's strategy, provide feedback, then watch her delegate to the team.
3. **Track your progress** — Watch your team take action from the or start new tasks in parallel.

## What you get out of the box

Your AI team comes pre-built with eight specialists, each ready to tackle different parts of your business.

- **Marketing** — Positioning, content, SEO, and platform-native social
- **Sales** — Outbound prospecting and competitive intel
- **Finance** — Cash forecasting, bookkeeping, and payroll review

## How your team works

Working with your AI team feels like messaging a colleague. Open Slack or Microsoft Teams, message Appy.AI, and describe what you need in plain language.

Violet reads your request and figures out which agent (or agents) should handle it, then routes the work automatically. Delegation flows up and down the org chart automatically: task assignments wake agents to start work, and cross-team requests delegate to the best agent for the job. If a task needs multiple specialists — like a blog post that needs writing, SEO optimization, and a header image — they coordinate behind the scenes.

Results come back to you where you sent the message. You can also set up agents to run autonomously on schedules, handling recurring tasks around the clock without you having to ask.
