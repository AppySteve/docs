Appy.AI gives you a complete AI team from day one — a fleet of specialized agents that work day and night to scale your business, right where your team already operates. No hiring, no training, no setup. Just tell them what you need and they get to work.

## Meet Violet — your Chief of Staff

Every team needs a leader. Violet is yours. She's the Chief of Staff who coordinates your entire AI team — receiving your goals, building a plan, and delegating work to the right specialists automatically.

Working with Violet follows three simple steps:

1. **Define the goal** — Manage business goals, not prompts. Just tell Violet what you need.
2. **Approve the plan** — Review Violet's strategy, provide feedback, then watch her delegate to the team.
3. **Track your progress** — Watch your team take action from the dashboard or start new tasks in parallel.

## What you get out of the box

Your AI team comes pre-built with eight specialists, each ready to tackle different parts of your business:

- **Senior SEO Strategist** — Analyzes your content and identifies opportunities to improve search rankings.
- **Competitive Researcher** — Tracks what your competitors are doing and surfaces insights you can act on.
- **Image Creation Specialist** — Generates visuals for your blog posts, social media, and marketing materials.
- **Content Strategist** — Plans and drafts content that aligns with your business goals.
- **Meeting Summarizer** — Turns meeting recordings into clear summaries with action items.
- **Brand Strategist** — Helps define and maintain a consistent brand voice across everything you create.
- **Sales Outreach Specialist** — Researches prospects and writes ready-to-send cold outreach emails.
- **Technical Writer** — Creates clear documentation, guides, and how-to content.

You can see your full roster anytime in the web platform under "Manage your agents."

## How your team works

Working with your AI team feels like messaging a colleague. Open Slack, message Appy.AI, and describe what you need in plain language.

Appy.AI figures out which agent (or agents) should handle your request and routes the work automatically. If a task needs multiple specialists — like a blog post that needs writing, SEO optimization, and a header image — they coordinate behind the scenes.

Results come back to you in Slack. You can also set up agents to run autonomously on schedules, handling recurring tasks without you having to ask.

## What you can do

Here's a taste of what your AI team can handle:

- Draft blog posts, emails, and social media content
- Research competitors and summarize what they're doing
- Summarize meetings and pull out action items
- Create images for your content and campaigns
- Write outreach emails for sales prospects

Want something done differently? Just tell Appy.AI in conversation. You can customize how agents work, teach them about your business, and even create entirely new agents when you need them.

For advanced configuration — like uploading knowledge files or setting up automated triggers — the web platform gives you full control.
