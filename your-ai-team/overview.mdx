# Overview

Appy.AI gives you a complete AI team from the moment you sign up. No setup, no configuration — just start delegating work.

## What you get out of the box

When you create your workspace, you get 8 specialist agents ready to work:

- **Senior SEO Strategist** — optimizes your content for search
- **Competitive Researcher** — tracks what your competitors are doing
- **Image Creation Specialist** — generates visuals for your content
- **Content Strategist** — plans and drafts written content
- **Meeting Summarizer** — turns meeting recordings into actionable notes
- **Brand Strategist** — keeps your messaging consistent
- **Sales Outreach Specialist** — crafts personalized outreach
- **Technical Writer** — creates documentation and how-to guides

You'll find these agents in a flat roster under "Manage your agents" in the web platform. They're ready to work immediately.

## How your team works

Message Appy.AI in Slack the same way you'd message a colleague. Describe what you need in plain language, and Appy.AI figures out which agent should handle it.

If a task needs multiple specialists, they coordinate automatically. One agent's output becomes another's input — you just get the final result back in Slack.

You don't need to know which agent handles what. Just describe the work, and your team takes it from there. Agents can also run autonomously on schedules or in the background, delivering results without you having to ask.

## What you can do with it

Delegate real business tasks like you would to a human team:

- "Draft a blog post about our new product launch"
- "Research our top 3 competitors and summarize what they're doing differently"
- "Summarize yesterday's team meeting and pull out the action items"
- "Create a LinkedIn post announcing our upcoming webinar"

Beyond day-to-day tasks, you can customize your team through conversation. Tell Appy.AI what's specific to your business — your brand voice, your target audience, your preferences — and agent behavior adjusts automatically.

Need a specialist that doesn't exist yet? Describe the role you need, and Appy.AI creates a new agent for you. No forms, no code — just conversation.
