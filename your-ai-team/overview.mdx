Appy.AI gives you a complete AI team from day one. No hiring, no training, no setup.

Your agents are ready to work the moment you connect Slack. Just message Appy.AI like you would a colleague, and your team handles the rest.

## What you get out of the box

Your AI team includes eight specialist agents, each with their own expertise:

- **Senior SEO Strategist** — Optimizes content for search engines and develops SEO strategies
- **Competitive Researcher** — Analyzes competitors and market trends
- **Image Creation Specialist** — Generates custom images and visual content
- **Content Strategist** — Plans and develops content strategies across channels
- **Meeting Summarizer** — Captures key points, action items, and decisions from meetings
- **Brand Strategist** — Develops brand positioning and messaging
- **Sales Outreach Specialist** — Crafts personalized outreach and follow-up messages
- **Technical Writer** — Creates clear documentation and technical content

All agents are immediately available in the "Manage your agents" section of the web platform. You don't need to configure or activate them.

<Info>
Your agents work together automatically. When you ask Appy.AI to complete a task, it figures out which agents to involve and coordinates them behind the scenes.
</Info>

## How your team works

Message Appy.AI in Slack using plain language. Describe what you need like you're talking to a real team member.

Appy.AI routes your request to the right agent or agents. If multiple specialists are needed, they coordinate automatically without you managing the handoffs.

Results come back to you in Slack. You can review, refine, or ask for changes just by replying.

Your agents can also run autonomously on schedules or triggers you set up. They'll notify you when work is complete.

## What you can do

Your AI team handles a wide range of work:

- Draft blog posts optimized for SEO
- Research competitors and summarize findings
- Summarize meetings and extract action items
- Create custom images for social media or presentations
- Write sales outreach emails tailored to prospects
- Develop content calendars and strategies
- Document technical processes and workflows

You can customize any agent through conversation. Tell Appy.AI what you need changed, and it updates the agent's behavior.

Need a specialist that doesn't exist yet? Create new agents on demand by describing what you need. Appy.AI will build them for you.

<Info>
For advanced configuration, use the web interface to adjust agent settings, manage permissions, and set up automation rules.
</Info>
