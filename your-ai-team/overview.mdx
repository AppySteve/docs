## Meet Violet — your Director of Appy.ai

Every team needs a leader. Violet is yours. She coordinates your entire AI team — translating your goals into plans, then delegating work to the right specialists automatically.

Working with Violet:

1. **Share your business** — Tell Violet what you're working on and what matters most.
2. **Approve the plan** — Review her strategy, give feedback, then watch her delegate.
3. **Track progress** — See your team take action, or kick off new tasks in parallel.

## How your team works

Working with your AI team feels like messaging a colleague. Open Slack or Microsoft Teams, describe what you need in plain language, and Violet routes the work to the right agent.

When a task needs multiple specialists — a blog post that needs writing, SEO, and a header image, for example — they coordinate behind the scenes. Results come back to you in the same thread where you asked.

You can also set up agents to run on schedules, handling recurring work around the clock without you having to ask.
