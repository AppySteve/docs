## Effortless Authentication

- **Multiple Login Options**: Subscribers can sign in with Google for instant access or use traditional email/password authentication.
- **Email Verification**: Automatic email verification ensures account security and reduces spam signups.
- **Password Recovery**: Built-in "forgot password" functionality means subscribers never get locked out of their accounts.
- **Professional Design**: Clean, friendly interface with engaging illustrations that build confidence and trust.

## Why This Matters for Your Business

- **Professional First Impression** - The polished onboarding experience immediately establishes credibility and builds confidence in your service.
- **Higher Conversion Rates** - Smooth, friction-free signup process reduces abandonment and increases trial-to-paid conversion.
- **Quality Data Collection** - Thoughtfully designed preference collection ensures you get the information needed for personalized agent interactions.
- **Reduced Support Burden** - Clear, self-explanatory onboarding means fewer "how do I sign up?" or "I can't access my account" support requests.
- **Enterprise-Grade Security** - Professional authentication and verification processes protect both you and your subscribers.
