## Tiers

[Appy.ai](http://Appy.ai) has three flat pricing tiers:

- Free - Perfect for testing out your idea and getting the ball rolling.
- Pro - For creators who are serious about building a thriving agent business.
  - \$29/month paid annually
  - \$39/month paid monthly
- Scale - Scale without limits and maximize revenue with [Appy.AI](http://Appy.AI)’s most advanced capabilities.
  - \$79/month paid annually
  - \$99/month paid monthly

  All new creators receive a one week free trial of the Scale plan

## Choosing the Right Plan

**Choose Free if:**

- You're testing the platform
- You need basic agent functionality
- You're building a proof of concept

**Choose Pro if:**

- You're ready to monetize your agents
- You need unlimited interactions
- You want Google Workspace integrations
- You're building 2-5 agents

**Choose Scale if:**

- You need unlimited agents
- You want full white-label capabilities
- You require advanced AI models
- You need business tool integrations
- You want priority support
