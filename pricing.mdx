## Choosing the Right Plan

**Choose Free if:**

- You're testing the platform
- You need basic agent functionality
- You're building a proof of concept

**Choose Pro if:**

- You will have people other than yourself using your agent
- You want more access to AI models
- You want priority support

**Choose Scale if:**

- You want to sell your agents
- You and your customers will use the agent regularly
- You want full white-label capabilities
- You require the most advanced AI models
- You want priority support
