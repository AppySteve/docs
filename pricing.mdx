[Appy.AI](http://Appy.ai) credits power all the work for Violet and her team. There are no add-ons, No per-seat charges, no fixed overhead for agents that aren't delivering value. Learn how many credits your team requires, and scale up when demand increases, scale back when it eases. 

## Get Started for Free

When you establish your team's [Appy.AI](http://Appy.AI) account, you start free with 40,000 credits (a \$100 value). Put your AI team to work with full capabilities.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are credits?">
    Credits represent the real cost of AI work. Credits aren't arbitrary units - they reflect actual costs to utilize LLMs through API, which Appy subsidizes so your team gets more output per dollar than you'd get going direct.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many credits does a task use?">
    It depends on what the task involves:

    - **Quick tasks** (summaries, lookups, short drafts, Slack responses): ~100–500 credits
    - **Research reports, multi-step workflows, longer deliverables**: ~1,000–3,000 credits

    Your credit usage broken down by task is available at [builder.appy.ai/billing](https://builder.appy.ai/billing).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are credits shared across my team?">
    Yes. Credits belong to the workspace. All team members draw from the same pool. Per-member and per-department limits are on the roadmap for a future update.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do credits roll over?">
    Monthly plan credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. Reward credits - earned through referrals and other programs - never expire and are utilized after monthly plan credits are exhausted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when credits run out?">
    Any in-progress task completes. Your workspace is notified and new tasks are paused until you upgrade to a higher plan tier or you reach the renewal of your billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add credits mid-cycle without changing plans?">
    To get more credits before your next renewal, upgrade to the next plan tier - you'll only owe the prorated difference at that moment, and you'll pay the full fee at the next cycle. Downgrading takes effect at the start of the next cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I monitor usage?">
    Credit usage — broken down by team member and task — is available at [builder.appy.ai/billing](https://builder.appy.ai/billing).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
