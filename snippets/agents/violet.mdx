## Meet Violet, your AI Chief of Staff

Violet is the one you actually talk to. She holds the picture of how your business runs — the tools, the rhythms, the open questions, the things that matter this week — and uses it to brief the right specialist for every job. Most AI assistants wait to be told what to do. Violet leads, suggests, and pushes back. She earns trust the way a great chief of staff does: by knowing your business well enough that her recommendations land.

She's not a chatbot. She's a peer.

### What she does

**Holds the picture.** Continuously builds a mental model of how your business operates — products, customers, tools, decisions in flight — so every brief lands with full context.

**Routes the work.** Hears what you're asking for, picks the right specialist, writes the brief, and brings the result back. You don't manage the handoff. She does.

**Asks the question worth asking.** When she needs to clarify, it's one sharp question — not a survey. Knowing what to ask is most of the value.

**Orchestrates the chain.** When a job needs Marcus to position, then Paige to write, then Maven to repurpose — Violet runs the sequence. You see the finished work.

**Hires, mentors, and fires.** Spin up bespoke specialists when a job doesn't fit the existing team. Update an agent's playbook in plain language and the change takes effect immediately. Archive what you're not using.

### What she delivers

- A coherent thread for everything your team produces — one place, one voice, one history
- Briefs, plans, and recommendations tied to specific specialist work
- Suggestions for what to do next, surfaced before you have to ask
- Quiet acknowledgment of the things you tell her, so the conversation feels heard
- The team's work, summarized in your context — not dumped raw

### Best with

Violet shines when she's plugged in. Integrations stop being setup chores and become the path to her becoming indispensable.

- **Where she lives** — Web, Slack, Microsoft Teams
- **Knowledge intake** — Gmail, Google Drive, OneDrive (so she can read what you read)
- **Calendar context** — Google Calendar, Microsoft Calendar
- **Everything your team connects** — accounting, CRM, payroll, support, sales tools all flow through Violet so she can route work to the right specialist

The more your team is plugged in, the more autonomously Violet can run.

### How it works

You talk to Violet the way you'd talk to a smart, opinionated chief of staff. She listens, asks one sharp question when she needs to, and either answers directly or delegates to a specialist. You approve plans, review work, and steer. She handles the rest.

### Try her with

- "Plan our launch week — figure out who needs to do what."
- "I have a board meeting Friday. Get me ready."
- "What does each of my team members do best?"
- "We just signed a new logo — coordinate the onboarding."
- "What should I be paying attention to this week?"
